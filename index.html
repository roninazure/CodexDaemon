Meet Dr. Jason Berner

With over 25 years of dedicated practice, Dr. Jason Berner has become one of North Georgia's most trusted endocrinologists, combining world-class training with genuine compassion for every patient.

After completing his medical education at Wayne State University School of Medicine in 1998, Dr. Berner pursued rigorous training at Emory University, completing both his Internal Medicine residency and Endocrinology fellowship at one of the nation's premier academic medical centers.

His expertise spans the full spectrum of endocrine disorders‚Äîfrom complex diabetes management using the latest insulin pump technology, to thyroid conditions, metabolic bone disease, and hormonal imbalances. Dr. Berner is particularly passionate about the intersection of cutting-edge technology and patient care.

"My involvement with Beta Bionics and the iLet Bionic Pancreas has been one of the proudest achievements of my career. Seeing how this technology transforms lives for people with Type 1 diabetes‚Äîgiving them freedom from constant calculations and worry‚Äîthat's why I became a physician."

Dr. Berner is affiliated with Piedmont Mountainside Hospital and Northside Hospital Cherokee, ensuring his patients have access to comprehensive care when needed.
